About Bloom3D

We're a San Jose-based 3D printing company serving local businesses with custom, high-quality prints. We partner with furniture stores, lifestyle brands, and small manufacturers to bring ideas to life—fast and affordably.

FAQs

What materials do you print with?

We support PLA, PETG, and ABS for a range of decor, mechanical, and heat-resistant applications.

Can I request specific colors or finishes?

Yes! We offer a variety of finishes and can order special spools on request for branding or aesthetics.

Do you offer design support?

We help refine STL files and can assist with parametric design for functionality or visual appeal.
